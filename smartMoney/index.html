Our Calculators

Cost of Interest

Understanding how much more you pay when using a credit card for a purchase can be difficult. The cost of interest calculator will show you how much you'll pay in total based on your purchase amount, your interest rate (APR), your monthly payment, and applicable sales tax!

Savings Growth

A high-interest savings account is a great way to make your money work for you. Use our savings growth calculator to compare interest rates, and to understand the importance of making regular monthly deposits.

Loan Payment Calculator

Understanding the relationship between amount borrowed, interest rate, and loan term is important when making decisions about borrowing money. Our payment calculator will help you estimate how much you'l wind up paying per month based on these factors.

Monthly Budgeting Calculator

Having a budget is one of the most important and fundamental concepts in personal finance. Use our monthly spending visualizer to identify where your money is going, how much is left over, and where you can save!
